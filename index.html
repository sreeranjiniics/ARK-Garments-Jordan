About Us – ARK Garment Manufacturing Industries Ltd.

Welcome to ARK Garment Manufacturing Industries Ltd., a leading garment manufacturer and exporter with over 30 years of experience. Established in 1995 in Sharjah, UAE, we have grown into a trusted global supplier of high-quality apparel, serving markets worldwide.

Who We Are

As a premier garment manufacturing and exporting company, we specialize in producing a wide range of clothing, from casual and formal wear to industrial uniforms. Our dedication to quality, innovation, and sustainability has made us a preferred choice for international brands, retailers, and corporate clients.

What We Do

Garment Manufacturing & Exporting

We produce and export high-quality apparel tailored to international fashion and industrial standards.

Private Label & Customization

We offer custom clothing solutions for global brands, ensuring top-tier fabrics, modern designs, and impeccable stitching.

Sustainable & Ethical Production

Our manufacturing processes prioritize sustainability, eco-friendly materials, and fair labor practices.

Stringent Quality Control

Every garment undergoes rigorous quality checks before export, ensuring durability and premium craftsmanship.
